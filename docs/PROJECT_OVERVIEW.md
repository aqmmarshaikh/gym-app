# Project Overview - Fitness Corner Gym

Fitness Corner Gym (FCG) is a mobile-first Progressive Web Application (PWA) that acts as a comprehensive **Gym Management System (GMS)**. The system is designed to digitalize gym operations completely, eliminating paperwork and manual logging for visitors, members, coaches, and owners.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience & Goals

### 1. Gym Visitors / Public Users
* Easily view gym opening hours, facilities, available machines, transformations, and frequently asked questions.
* Join the gym immediately by submitting a digital registration request which builds a WhatsApp chat thread with gym sales teams.

### 2. Gym Members
* Authenticate seamlessly using a unique Membership Code.
* View a dashboard tracking daily subscription status, water intake, calories, and assigned coach details.
* Scan a digital membership QR Code at the front desk for contact-free entry.
* Complete daily workout programs customized to their goal.
* Record body measurements and view progress charts.

### 3. Gym Coaches
* Check in members by scanning their digital membership card QR codes or through a manual lookup override.
* Configure gym machines, availability statuses, workout categories, and design weekly plans.
* Register new members and manage subsciptions.

### 4. Gym Owner
* Monitor overall gym metrics, active members, active coaches, and fee collections.
* Manage coaches, override passwords, and suspend staff accounts.
* Customize gym branding settings (name, tagline, contact information).
* Instantly download secure JSON data backups of the entire gym database.
